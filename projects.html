Tempo Training


This web app creates Spotify playlists based on the user's input regarding step cadence & artist preference. This Heroku-deployed, python-based app uses Spotify APIs to retrieve recommended tracks based on this information. The resulting playlist consists of tracks of a similar tempo, designed to complement one's running training.
